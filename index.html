About This Game

Star Force is an interstellar strategy game where tactical mastery, fleet-building, and real-time space combat collide. Developed by Fusion Glow Studios, this thrilling game immerses you in a vast sci-fi universe filled with galactic conquests and legendary commanders. Build your ultimate fleet, conquer the battlefield, and ascend to power as the supreme leader of the cosmos!
